# COVID Impact on Banking Sector (NIFTY Bank Analysis)

---

## 1. Objective

Analyze how the banking sector behaved before, during, and after COVID-19 using historical market data.

The goal is not just visualization, but to:

* Establish **baseline market behavior (Pre-COVID)**
* Measure the **severity and speed of the COVID crash**
* Evaluate the **strength and stability of recovery**

---

## 2. Core Idea

The project studies market behavior across three distinct phases:

* **Pre-COVID (Before March 2020)** → Normal market conditions
* **COVID Phase (March 2020 – Dec 2021)** → Shock and volatility
* **Post-COVID (2022 onwards)** → Recovery and trend continuation

Each phase is analyzed independently and then compared.

---

## 3. Data Used

* NIFTY Bank historical index data (CSV)
* Fields:

  * Date
  * Close Price

---

## 4. Approach

### Phase-Based Analysis

Instead of treating the dataset as a whole, the data is split into:

* Pre-COVID
* COVID
* Post-COVID

Each phase is analyzed using **custom metrics designed for that phase’s purpose**.

---

## 5. Metrics Computed

### Pre-COVID (Baseline Behavior)

* Baseline Growth
* Volatility (normal market stability)
* Typical Drawdown (normal corrections)
* Stability Ratio (time spent near highs)
* Trend Consistency (upward movement frequency)

---

### COVID Phase (Shock Analysis)

* Crash Drawdown (severity of fall)
* Crash Duration (speed of fall)
* Recovery % (bounce from bottom)
* Volatility Spike
* Downtrend Ratio (panic pressure)
* Uptrend Ratio (recovery behavior)

---

### Post-COVID (Recovery Analysis)

* Recovery vs Pre-COVID Peak
* Post-COVID Growth
* Volatility (stability after recovery)
* Drawdown (corrections during recovery)
* Uptrend Ratio (trend strength)
